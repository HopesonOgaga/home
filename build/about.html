about us

11th Hour Innovation Properties LTD is a property sourcing based in London, UK. Our main focus is sourcing properties for Supported living, Care home provider, assisted living, and properties management. Additionally, we assist landlords in securing a guaranteed rent for their property through company lets, which can include Local authority contracts and Supported living arrangements. Since our establishment in early 2022, we strive to become the leading providers of company let properties throughout the UK.
