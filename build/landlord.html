Option 2: Supported Living Companies - Stock type: 3 bed + house

We Source housing for Supported Living Companies. These companies would rent your property from you on a GUARANTEE RENT for a long-term tenancy, dealing with the tenants, maintenance and bills for the property throughout the duration. They work along with the council & government to house vulnerable tenant groups and provide secure housing for them. They would always have at least 1 staff member in the property to ensure all tenants are being looked after. The tenant’s groups vary between a wide range from 16- 25 young adults to the elderly. They only look for large houses usually 4 bedrooms upwards and are registered and verified by the CQC which is the professional body for supported living. These companies are funded by the government, which is why they can provide GUARANTEED RENT and, on most cases, can match your asking price.

1. Why does option 1 not pay deposits..?

Option 1 Business model works in conjunction with the home office & local council, their income to us is provided 2 months in arrears, which mean each property they take on, they have to fund the first 2 months from their capital. They provide a soft maintenance lease which means in the contract it stipulates that they are full responsibility for any internal repairs & maintenance throughout the duration of the contract and must hand the property back in the same condition it was let to them (based on the inventory check before move in) They have multiple properties on management and multiple units they acquire on a monthly bases due to Cashflow they don’t provide deposit as our capital needs to be used across multiple units to offer rent in advance

    2. What is the process to complete a let..?

  • (For Local Authority Options) We do not require deposits, given our frequent property acquisitions that necessitate continual capital for rental payments.
